Privacy Policy

TimeRace - Redmine Tracker
Last Updated: February 2025

Overview

TimeRace - Redmine Tracker is a Chrome extension that helps you track your work hours and compete with your team through gamified leaderboards. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data We Collect

1. Redmine API Credentials

  • What: Your Redmine server URL and API key
  • Where it's stored: Locally in your browser's Chrome storage
  • Purpose: To communicate with your Redmine instance and retrieve your time tracking data

2. Time Tracking Data

  • What: Logged hours, expected hours, and team ranking information
  • Where it's stored: Temporarily cached in your browser's local storage
  • Purpose: To display your progress and team leaderboards

How We Use Your Data

Your Redmine credentials are used only to communicate with your Redmine instance.

  • We do not send your data to any third-party servers
  • We do not collect analytics or usage statistics
  • We do not track your browsing activity

Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API
  • Your API key is never transmitted outside of your browser except to your configured Redmine server
  • Data is encrypted at rest by Chrome's built-in security mechanisms

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone.

  • No third-party data sharing
  • No data sales to advertisers
  • No analytics or tracking services

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you:

  • Uninstall the extension (Chrome will clear all locally stored data)
  • Manually clear your browser data/extension data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data through Chrome's extension settings
  • Delete your data at any time by clearing extension data or uninstalling
  • Opt-out by simply not using the extension

Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store.

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with Chrome Web Store requirements and general data protection regulations.
